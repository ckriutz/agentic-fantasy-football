# Fantasy Football League Configuration

## Scoring Format

- **Type**: Full PPR (Point Per Reception)
- **Scoring System**: Standard fantasy football scoring with 1 point awarded per reception
- **Common On**: ESPN, Yahoo Fantasy Sports, Sleeper

## League Structure

### Roster Composition

| Position | Count | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| QB | 1 | Quarterback |
| RB | 2 | Running Backs |
| WR | 2 | Wide Receivers |
| TE | 1 | Tight End |
| FLEX | 1 | RB/WR/TE (any of three) |
| K | 1 | Kicker |
| DEF | 1 | Defense/Special Teams |
| **Bench** | **6** | Reserve players |
| **Total Roster Size** | **17** | |

### Draft Format

- **Type**: Snake Draft (alternating order per round)
- **Number of Teams**: 10
- **Total Picks**: 170 (10 teams × 17 rounds)

## Scoring Settings

### Passing Statistics
- **Pass Reception**: +1 pt per reception
- **Pass Yards**: +0.04 pts per yard (1 pt per 25 yards)
- **Passing Touchdowns**: +4 pts
- **Interceptions**: -1 pt

### Rushing & Receiving
- **Rushing Yards**: +0.1 pts per yard (1 pt per 10 yards)
- **Rushing Touchdowns**: +6 pts
- **Receiving Yards**: +0.1 pts per yard (1 pt per 10 yards)
- **Receiving Touchdowns**: +6 pts
- **Fumbles Lost**: -2 pts

### Defense/Special Teams
- **Sack**: +1 pt
- **Interception**: +2 pts
- **Fumble Recovery**: +2 pts
- **Defensive Touchdown**: +6 pts
- **Shutout (≤6 pts allowed)**: +10 pts
- **Points Allowed**: Scaled (-1 to -10 pts depending on total)

### Kicker
- **Field Goal (0-39 yds)**: +3 pts
- **Field Goal (40-49 yds)**: +4 pts
- **Field Goal (50+ yds)**: +5 pts
- **Extra Point**: +1 pt

## League Rules

- **Roster Lock**: Locked at NFL game start time (no mid-game changes)
- **Waiver Wire**: Rolling waiver priority (priority resets after successful claim)
- **Trade Deadline**: Ends when playoffs start (Week 15)
- **Playoff Structure**: 6 teams, 3-week championship bracket
- **Bench**: Can hold injured reserve players for strategic depth

## Season Overview

- **Regular Season**: 14 weeks (Weeks 1-14 of NFL season)
- **Playoffs**: Weeks 15-17 (6 teams compete)
- **Champion**: Team with highest total points in championship week
